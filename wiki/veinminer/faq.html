Frequently Asked Questions

What is VeinMiner?

VeinMiner is an Add-On for Minecraft Bedrock Edition that allows you to cut down entire trees by breaking just the bottom block. It significantly speeds up the process of gathering wood and clearing forests.

Does VeinMiner work with all types of trees?

VeinMiner works with all vanilla Minecraft trees and many custom trees from supported Add-Ons. You can configure which tree types are affected in the settings.

How do I configure VeinMiner?

Configure VeinMiner using the in-game configuration book. Craft it by combining a regular book with any sapling in a crafting grid.

Is VeinMiner compatible with other Add-Ons?

Yes, VeinMiner is designed to be compatible with many popular Minecraft Add-Ons. Check the Compatibility page for a list of tested Add-Ons.
