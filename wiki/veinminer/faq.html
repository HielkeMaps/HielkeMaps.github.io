Frequently Asked Questions

What is VeinMiner?

VeinMiner is an Add-On for Minecraft Bedrock Edition that allows you to mine entire ore veins by breaking just a single block. It significantly speeds up the process of gathering resources and mining large ore deposits.

How do I configure VeinMiner?

Configure VeinMiner using the in-game configuration book. Craft it by combining a regular book with an iron ingot in a crafting grid.

Does VeinMiner work with enchantments?

Yes! VeinMiner fully supports Fortune for increased drops and Unbreaking to reduce tool damage. When using Silk Touch, the ore blocks themselves will drop instead of their resources.

Is VeinMiner compatible with other Add-Ons?

Yes, VeinMiner is designed to be compatible with many popular Minecraft Add-Ons. Check the Compatibility page for a list of tested Add-Ons.
