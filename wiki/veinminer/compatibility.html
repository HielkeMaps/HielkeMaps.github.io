VeinMiner is designed to work seamlessly with various Minecraft Add-Ons. Below is a list of known compatible Add-Ons.

Add-On Name Creator Remarks
Tinker's Construct FTB Studios Compatible with Tinker's pickaxes and ores.
More Ores & Tools Netherpixel Full support for all custom ores and pickaxes.
Weapons+ 4K Studios All custom pickaxes are supported.
Note: Compatibility is based on the latest versions of both VeinMiner and the listed Add-Ons. Always ensure you're using the most recent updates for the best experience.
Note: Is your Add-On compatible but not on the list? Let me know by contacting me, and I'll be sure to add it here :)
