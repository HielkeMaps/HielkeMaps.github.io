Frequently Asked Questions

What is TreeCapitator?

TreeCapitator is an Add-On for Minecraft Bedrock Edition that allows you to cut down entire trees by breaking just the bottom block. It significantly speeds up the process of gathering wood and clearing forests.

Does TreeCapitator work with all types of trees?

TreeCapitator works with all vanilla Minecraft trees and many custom trees from supported Add-Ons. You can configure which tree types are affected in the settings.

How do I configure TreeCapitator?

Configure TreeCapitator using the in-game configuration book. Craft it by combining a regular book with any sapling in a crafting grid.

Is TreeCapitator compatible with other Add-Ons?

Yes, TreeCapitator is designed to be compatible with many popular Minecraft Add-Ons. Check the Compatibility page for a list of tested Add-Ons.
